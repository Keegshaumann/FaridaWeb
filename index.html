Cajee Botes Orthotist & Prosthetist

Restoring Movement. Supporting Independence.

Cajee Botes Orthotist & Prosthetist provides individualised, clinician-led orthotic and prosthetic care across South Africa. Led by qualified Orthotist and Prosthetist Farida Cajee-Botes, we deliver mobile home and hospital assessments so that rehabilitation can begin early — bringing professional, assessment-led care directly to you following surgery, injury, or illness.

Our Services

  • Custom Orthotics — individually designed upper- and lower-limb, spinal, and paediatric orthotic devices crafted to your biomechanical needs.
  • Off-the-Shelf Orthotics — prefabricated braces and supports for injury management, post-operative recovery, and sports protection.
  • Mobility Aids — crutches, walking frames, wheelchairs, and bathroom support to help you move safely and independently.
  • Medical Compression — medical-grade compression garments for lymphoedema, oedema control, scar therapy, and post-surgical recovery.
  • Breast & Silicone Prosthetics — breast, facial, finger, and toe prosthetics fitted with dignity and a natural appearance.
  • Upper & Lower Limb Prosthetics — prosthetic limb fitting, paediatric prosthetic care, and ongoing adjustment and support.

Why Choose Us

  • Individualised, assessment-led care tailored to your diagnosis and daily living.
  • Clinician-led expertise from a qualified orthotist and prosthetist.
  • Mobile home and hospital visits for early, accessible rehabilitation.
  • Functional, thoughtful device design focused on long-term independence.
