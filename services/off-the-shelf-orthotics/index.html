Off-the-Shelf Orthotics

Conservative orthotic support for pain management and injury recovery

What is it?

Conservative orthotic support includes clinically prescribed braces, supports, taping techniques, and soft orthotic devices to manage pain, improve joint stability, and support recovery without surgery.

How can it help?

  • Managing musculoskeletal pain
  • Supporting injured joints during healing
  • Improving functional stability
  • Reducing strain during movement
  • Supporting recovery after injury or surgery
  • Improving tolerance to activity

Devices We Provide

A selection of the devices we fit within this service. Filter by area of the body to find what fits your needs — the right device is always confirmed after an individual assessment.

Knee

Osteoarthritis Offloading Knee Brace

Adults with mild-to-moderate one-sided knee osteoarthritis seeking pain relief during standing and walking.

A prefabricated hinged or wrap-style brace that applies a gentle three-point leverage system to shift load away from the worn side of the knee joint. Fitted from standard off-the-shelf sizes and adjusted to the individual's alignment, it is designed to help ease osteoarthritic pain during daily activity or sport.

  • Adjustable offloading strap/hinge system
  • Suits medial or lateral compartment involvement
  • Low-profile design for daily wear or sport
  • Available in multiple standard sizes
Knee

Hinged Ligament Knee Brace

Active adults, athletes and children with knee ligament instability who need side support during daily activity or sport.

A prefabricated hinged brace with rigid side supports that resists excessive side-to-side or rotational movement while allowing normal bending and straightening. Fitted off the shelf in a range of adult and paediatric sizes, it is intended to protect a ligament-deficient or previously injured knee during activity.

  • Anatomical hinge tracks the knee's natural motion
  • Limits excessive lateral and rotational movement
  • Adjustable straps for fit and compression
  • Paediatric sizing available
Knee

Post-Operative ROM Knee Brace

Patients recovering from knee surgery or significant soft-tissue injury who need controlled, progressive range-of-motion protection.

A prefabricated hinged brace with adjustable range-of-motion stops that protects the knee and supports a gradual, clinician-guided return of movement after surgery or injury, with an option to lock the joint fully immobile. Straps are easy to readjust as post-surgical swelling changes.

  • Adjustable ROM hinge stops
  • Full immobilisation/lock option
  • Easy-adjust straps to accommodate swelling
  • Lightweight, washable materials
Knee

Patellar Stabilising Knee Brace

People with kneecap tracking problems, subluxation, dislocation, or anterior knee pain who need lateral support during activity.

A prefabricated brace with an adjustable buttress pad or targeted strap that helps guide the kneecap's tracking and resists lateral movement during activity. Fitted off the shelf, it is intended to support the patellofemoral joint and ease anterior knee pain during sport or daily movement.

  • Adjustable lateral buttress pad or tracking strap
  • Low-profile, breathable materials
  • Heat-mouldable hinge stays on select models
  • Allows full knee range of motion
Knee

Elastic Knee Support Sleeve

People wanting light compression and support for a mildly painful or swollen knee during sport or daily activity.

A knitted elastic sleeve that provides gentle compression and warmth around the knee joint, with light padding at the sides for added support. It is a soft, off-the-shelf alternative to a rigid brace, worn for everyday comfort once a knee condition has moved beyond the acute stage.

  • Elastic knitted fabric with gentle compression
  • Does not restrict normal knee motion
  • Breathable, machine-washable material
  • Simple pull-on fit, no straps required
Ankle & Foot

Ankle Stirrup Support Brace

People with ankle sprains, chronic instability, or wanting preventive support during sport.

A semi-rigid prefabricated brace with rigid side stays and adjustable strapping that limits excessive inward/outward ankle movement while still allowing normal walking motion. Fitted off the shelf in standard sizes, it is designed to fit inside most footwear.

  • Semi-rigid stirrup shell or side stays
  • Figure-8 or lace-up strapping options
  • Fits inside most standard footwear
  • Available in multiple sizes, no moulding required
Ankle & Foot

Walker/Fracture Boot

Patients with ankle or foot fractures, severe sprains, or post-surgical needs requiring rigid immobilisation while remaining mobile.

A prefabricated rigid-shell boot that holds the ankle and foot in a controlled position, with a rocker sole to support a smoother, more stable gait during healing. Adjustable air or foam bladders and straps are fitted from a standard size range rather than individually moulded.

  • Rigid shell with adjustable straps and air/foam liners
  • Rocker outsole for a more natural walking pattern
  • Available in multiple heights and sizes
  • Removable for wound care or therapy as advised
Ankle & Foot

Off-the-Shelf AFO for Drop Foot

Patients with drop foot from neurological or muscular weakness who need help clearing the foot while walking.

A prefabricated ankle-foot orthosis or dynamic strap device that assists dorsiflexion during the swing phase of walking, helping the foot clear the ground and reducing tripping risk. Made from flexible carbon, composite or semi-rigid material, it is selected and sized to the patient without individual moulding.

  • Semi-rigid or dynamic-strap dorsiflexion assist
  • Low-profile design fits inside standard footwear
  • Flexible construction stores and returns energy on select models
  • Available in several stiffness/size options
Ankle & Foot

Night Splint (Plantar/Achilles)

Patients with plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendinopathy or chronic calf tightness who need a sustained overnight stretch.

A prefabricated splint worn during sleep that holds the ankle and foot in a neutral or gently dorsiflexed position to maintain a steady stretch of the plantar fascia and Achilles tendon overnight. Fitted off the shelf, it is intended to support recovery from heel and calf-related pain rather than provide daytime mobility.

  • Adjustable dorsiflexion angle
  • Padded, lightweight shell
  • Hook-and-loop closure for comfort adjustment
Neck

Cervical Collar (Rigid, Adjustable)

Patients requiring neck immobilisation after injury or surgery, or during emergency care; adult and paediatric sizing available.

A prefabricated rigid collar that limits cervical spine movement while relieving pressure at the chin, back of the head and shoulders. Adjustable height settings allow it to be fitted to the individual, with paediatric and emergency-use variants available.

  • Adjustable height settings
  • Contoured chin and occipital padding for pressure relief
  • Tracheal opening for airway access
  • Paediatric and emergency-use versions available
Hip

Hernia Support Belt

For adults with a reducible inguinal or groin hernia needing comfortable support during daily activity.

A prefabricated support belt with a targeted compression pad that holds a reducible groin hernia gently supported during standing, walking and daily activity. It eases discomfort and the dragging sensation while surgical review is awaited or not appropriate — it does not repair the hernia itself.

  • Targeted, adjustable compression pad
  • Single- and double-sided options
  • Low-profile fit under clothing
  • Supports symptoms — does not repair the hernia
Spine & Back

Maternity Support Belt

For pregnancy-related lower back and pelvic girdle pain, providing gentle support as the abdomen grows.

A prefabricated maternity belt that gently lifts and supports the abdomen while stabilising the lower back and pelvis, easing strain during standing, walking and daily activity through pregnancy. Adjustable panels adapt to the body's changing shape in each trimester.

  • Gentle abdominal lift with lower-back support
  • Adjustable panels adapt through each trimester
  • Breathable, low-profile fit under clothing
  • Eases pregnancy-related back and pelvic girdle strain
Spine & Back

Abdominal Binder (Post-Operative)

For support after abdominal surgery, including caesarean section, and for gentle support of abdominal hernias.

A prefabricated elasticated binder that applies even, comfortable compression around the abdomen to support healing tissue after surgery, ease movement-related pain and provide gentle support over an abdominal hernia. Adjustable closures let the fit change as post-surgical swelling settles.

  • Even, adjustable abdominal compression
  • Supports recovery after abdominal surgery and caesarean section
  • Gentle support for abdominal hernias
  • Breathable panels for extended daily wear
Spine & Back

Rigid Lumbosacral Orthosis (LSO)

Patients needing significant immobilisation and pain relief for the lumbar spine after injury, surgery, or for degenerative conditions.

A prefabricated, low-profile rigid orthosis with contoured anterior and posterior panels that restricts lumbar spine movement to relieve pain and support healing. Adjustable closures accommodate changes in swelling or fit during recovery.

  • Contoured rigid panels for gross lumbar immobilisation
  • Adjustable closure to accommodate swelling
  • Low-profile design for wear under clothing
  • No custom moulding required
Wrist & Hand

Wrist Splint (Day & Night Support)

Patients with wrist sprains, tendon strain, or carpal tunnel symptoms needing daytime support or overnight rest of the wrist.

A prefabricated splint with a removable rigid or semi-rigid palmar stay that holds the wrist in a neutral or supported position, easing strain on the joint and tendons. Worn during the day for general wrist support or overnight to reduce pressure on the median nerve for those with carpal tunnel symptoms, it leaves the fingers free for hand use.

  • Removable rigid/semi-rigid stay holds a neutral position
  • Adjustable hook-and-loop straps for a secure fit
  • Breathable, low-profile fabric
  • No custom moulding required
Wrist & Hand

Thumb Spica Splint

Patients with thumb ligament sprains, joint irritation, or post-injury protection needs at the base of the thumb.

A prefabricated splint that stabilises the thumb and its base joint while leaving the rest of the hand free to move. Fitted off the shelf in standard sizes, it is designed to relieve pain and protect the joint from ligament sprain or tendon irritation while healing progresses.

  • Rigid or semi-rigid thumb stay
  • Adjustable strapping for individual fit
  • Leaves fingers and wrist free to move
  • Low-profile, comfortable for daily wear
Elbow

Elbow Support / Epicondylitis Strap

Patients with tennis or golfer's elbow, or general elbow strain, wanting counterforce support during activity.

A prefabricated compression sleeve or counterforce strap that reduces strain on the forearm tendons at the elbow during activity. Available as a broader compression sleeve for general elbow strain or a focused counterforce strap for targeted relief of tennis or golfer's elbow symptoms.

  • Adjustable counterforce strap or compression sleeve options
  • Focused pressure pad reduces tendon strain
  • Breathable, lightweight materials
  • Easy to apply and remove for daily use
Shoulder

Shoulder Immobiliser Sling

Patients recovering from shoulder surgery, such as rotator cuff repair, or significant shoulder injury needing positioned immobilisation.

A prefabricated sling system that supports the weight of the arm and holds the shoulder in a controlled neutral or abducted position to protect healing tissue. Optional abduction or external-rotation pillow attachments allow the position to be adjusted for different recovery protocols.

  • Adjustable arm sling with ergonomic strapping
  • Optional rigid abduction pillow
  • Optional external-rotation pillow
  • Breathable padding to reduce pressure points
Elbow

Elbow ROM Brace

For patients recovering from elbow surgery, fracture or significant soft-tissue injury who need protected, gradually increasing elbow movement.

A prefabricated hinged elbow brace with adjustable range-of-motion stops that control how far the elbow can bend and straighten while healing. The permitted range is set by the clinician and progressively opened as recovery advances, with padded cuffs securing the brace to the upper arm and forearm.

  • Adjustable ROM hinge with flexion/extension stops
  • Lockable at a fixed angle where required
  • Padded, adjustable upper-arm and forearm cuffs
  • Lightweight frame fitted off the shelf
Spine & Back

Posture Support Brace (Thoracic)

For rounded-shoulder posture and upper-back muscular strain.

A prefabricated posture-correcting brace gently draws the shoulders back and supports the thoracic spine in a more upright alignment, easing the muscular strain behind upper-back and postural pain. It acts as a supportive reminder to sit and stand taller rather than permanently altering spinal structure.

  • Draws the shoulders into a more upright posture
  • Eases upper-back muscular strain
  • Lightweight and wearable under clothing
  • Adjustable, low-profile fit
